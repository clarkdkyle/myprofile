About Me

Throughout my professional career the majority of my time was used engaging with nerds and do nerd things. I love video games, comic books, board games, and movies. I spent most of my roaring twenties running an all in one nerd hobby shop called Game Nut Entertainment, that specialized in movies, video games, board games, card games, and comic books. What I love most about being a nerd is engaging with people and seeing how excited they get about their favorite topics. One of the most frustrating things about being a person is never having enough time for you personal interests.

With this page I aim to organize the games I want to play next. As you'll see I have too many games I want to play and not enough time. I aim to keep a list of games I want to play next.
